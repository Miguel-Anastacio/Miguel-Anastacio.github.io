About Me

Hi! I'm Miguel Anastácio, a Game Developer/Software Developer from Lisbon, Portugal.

I graduated from Abertay University in Computer Games and Applications Development in 2023. I am passionate about game development specifically and software development in general. I have experience with game engines such as Unreal and Unity.

At the moment, I am working as a Embedded Software Developer at Critical Techworks. Where I develop software for the central ECU of BMW cars. But my true passion is Game Development. In my spare time I develop and publish plugins for the Unreal Engine.
